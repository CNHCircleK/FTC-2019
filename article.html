8 Mysteries of FTC : Solved!

Where does the cost go?

The FTC Registration cost covers 2 nights in the cabins, four delicious meals, and everything that comes with a weekend getaway in the woods!

What is FTC?

Fall Training Conference is one of the largest District Events hosted by CNH Circle K. The schedule is packed tight with activities that will help you build new friendships and develop your leadership skills! You’ll also have the chance to meet members from all across the Cali-Nev-Ha District.

Why do so many people go to FTC?

It’s no mystery. With close to 700 attendees each year, there are so many opportunities for growth, networking, and long-lasting memories. FTC is one of the greatest experiences any member will encounter in Circle K.

Why does FTC have a theme,
what does it have to do with the event?

FTC has a different theme every year that gives it a fresh, new feel that becomes closely integrated with numerous aspects of the event. This year, our theme is Mystery! You will see elements of Mystery in the event’s publicity (graphics, promotional videos, etc.), on-site decorations, and much more!

How is the food?

3 meals are provided on Saturday, and breakfast is provided on Sunday, all buffet-style! Specific food prepared by the campsite varies from year to year, but it always leaves members coming back for more!

What is a “workshop”,
and what kind of workshops can I expect?

A workshop is a mini lesson hosted by fellow CKI members, alumni, and/or advisors to help spread knowledge on a particular topic based on their personal experiences. Workshops vary each year, but definitely expect to see workshops pertaining to the three tenets, college, real life situations, and some fun ones as well!

Where do we stay?

Within the fee that you pay, there are designated cabins for members to stay at. The females will be in a separate cabin from the males, but members will be assigned in the same room with other members of their school.

Will I have time to do work?

FTC is packed full of back-to-back activities, so periods of free time for studying are very limited! Keep in mind that you paid ~$150 and travel all this way for a reason - make the most of your experience and participate!
